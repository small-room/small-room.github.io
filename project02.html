Hannah Benwell is a photographer who works in wedding and portrait photography. She captures the moment and what it is about, so the branding and stationary needed to capture her.

The logo is a simple take on a viewfinder incorporated with her name, it is dynamic and can adapt to wherever it is applied.

The burgundy colour is that of Hannah’s hair, which was applied for extra character and a personal touch.
